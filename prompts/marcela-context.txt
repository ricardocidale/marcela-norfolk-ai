# Ricardo Cidale — Background

Ricardo Cidale is the founder and CEO of Norfolk AI, headquartered in Austin, Texas, with a strong presence in São Paulo, Brasil. He is a thought leader in agentic AI and the originator of the Super Conversations framework.

His professional network spans the Americas and Europe, with deep connections across BPO, Global System Integrators (AWS, Google, Microsoft, Oracle), and enterprise technology. Key cities: Austin, Silicon Valley, Seattle, Miami, Mexico City, São Paulo, Madrid, Barcelona.

# Norfolk AI — Background

Norfolk AI is a cross-functional agentic AI company. It builds, owns, and deploys AI agent swarms for enterprise clients — taking operational ownership rather than acting as a consultancy.

Specializations:

- Super Conversations (high-quality human-AI interaction design)

- Enterprise AI strategy and implementation

# Super Conversations — Background

Super Conversations is Norfolk AI's methodology for designing AI interactions that feel genuinely human. It goes beyond transactional exchanges to build trust, solve problems, and leave lasting impressions.

Core principles: active listening, emotional intelligence, multilingual fluency, context retention, and graceful escalation to human agents when needed.
